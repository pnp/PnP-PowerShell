#Get-SPOTaxonomyItem
Returns a taxonomy item
##Syntax
```powershell
Get-SPOTaxonomyItem
        -TermPath <String>
```


##Parameters
Parameter|Type|Required|Description
---------|----|--------|-----------
|TermPath|String|True|The path, delimited by | of the taxonomy item to retrieve, alike GROUPLABEL|TERMSETLABEL|TERMLABEL|
